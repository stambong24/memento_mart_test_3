white rabbit

white rabbit candies are small milk candies, wrapped in an edible paper made of rice paper. originating from china in the 1940s, these candies are simple, sweet, and nostalgic. In fact, it’s a favourite of many all around asia as well. For those unfamiliar with it, the texture is similar to that of a tootsie roll. But white rabbit’s popularity goes beyond the taste. Its packaging and branding is as iconic as it is tasty. The colours and design are like a glimpse into the past.
